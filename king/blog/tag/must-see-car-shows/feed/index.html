]]> September’s featured car show has grown so large over its history that the event actually spans four days, two times each year – once in April and again in September.
Charlotte Auto Fair – September 18-21, Charlotte, NC
Now in its 38th year, the Charlotte Auto Fair has been organized by the Hornets Nest Region since 1978, and is held at the Charlotte Motor Speedway. With more than 150,000 attendees, this monster of a car show boasts over 6,000 auto vendors and has a Car Corral that offers roughly 1,600 classic cars for auction. Events and vehicle displays are broken up into four major fields both inside and outside of the speedway itself, and include groupings of cars from over 50 other regional and national car clubs. For more information about the Charlotte Auto Fair, click here to find out about ticket information, event schedules, and Car Corral requirements.
