January is the North American International Auto Show in Detroit and of course in Scottsdale, Arizona the wonderful world of Barrett-Jackson where you can drool over rows and rows, tents and tents full of rare, and beautiful cars. The Chicago Auto Show follows in February, and the New York International Auto Show closes out the season in April. In addition to these four major U.S. auto shows, there are also a number of smaller auto shows in cities throughout the country. Get your motor running and your bank account flush but consider this before buying or consigning- outrageous fees and commissions.
Televised auctions are costly and have buyers fees attached to them. BJ, Mecum, and the like are for people who want the prestige of having bought from a televised auction.   The good ones go for top dollar and you’ll be paying an exorbitant amount on travel, commissions, taxes, and transport costs.  Equal cars can be found MUCH cheaper via private sale.  Get your fill of thrills at any or all of these auction and car shows but leave knowing there is a better deal out there.  And when you do decide to buy use PaySAFE® an online closing table providing buyers and sellers a convenient one-stop location to create, negotiate and close trades with financial protection.
